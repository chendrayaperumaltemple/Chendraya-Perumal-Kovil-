History of the Temple

Chendraya Perumal Temple, built 300 years ago, was recently reconstructed by Jaisakthi P.K. Sekar and the local community. The temple now features three shrines: Chendraya Perumal, Sri Devi Budevi / Vinayagar, and Varahi Amman.
